How it Works
  1. Allow CloudOffset to access your AWS Account via a CloudFormation template. If you are using an organization, allow us access to the Master Organization. We only get access to CostExplorer and listing your organizations accounts, you can review the exact policy we use.
  2. We'll scan your account with an AWS Lambda function from our account (We're carbon-nuetral via us-west-2!)
  3. We crunch the numbers and let you know your average offset cost over the last 3 months.
  4. You find a Carbon Offset provider or use one of our suggestions!
